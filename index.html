About Us

Nestec Tea Limited, located in the vibrant port city of Mombasa, Kenya, is a dynamic company specializing in various aspects of the tea industry. Established with a vision to streamline tea logistics, Nestec has grown to become a leading name in tea freight forwarding, ensuring that local and international clients receive their tea products efficiently and in optimal condition. Leveraging Mombasa's strategic position as a major tea export hub, the company offers comprehensive freight services that cater to the unique demands of tea transportation, from handling delicate cargo to navigating complex shipping regulations.

Extensive services offered include

Straight-line supply

Straight-line supply through buying tea from local farmers and producers, fostering strong relationships with the Kenyan tea community

Blending and packaging

Blending and packaging where traditional techniques combined with modern technology to create a variety of tea products.

specialized advisory services

Offering specialized advisory services to tea growers and businesses designed to optimize tea production and improve quality.
